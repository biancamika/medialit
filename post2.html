Problems that the Young Generation Has Today

There has been a realization that every student has troubles.

Posted by Kei Gonzales on April 4, 2018

There has been a realization that every student has troubles. What is the cause? Stress. Stress is the key factor that happens when your body can no longer handle work. This also does in fact happen to adults. As most people know, stress can kill you. According to a study, there has been more stress on children in high school today than what people had before. Another study shows that students in high school need 7-8 hours of sleep to function. Unfortunately, there are people who work past midnight to finish studying.

People balancing many responsibilities have been stressed. It is caused by many factors. It should be understood that students no longer have time to accomplish everything. A simple solution to this is giving more time to students. However, there are many problems that come in with even more problematic solutions. That may in the end cause a deadly disaster. In the end nothing wil happen if we don’t take action now. How can we improve if we can’t do what is right?
